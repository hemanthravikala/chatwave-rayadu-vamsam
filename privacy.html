Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Messages on this platform are NOT end-to-end encrypted. Ideally this service is for casual family communication only.

1. Purpose of this Website

Rayaduvamsam Chat is a private, personal website created exclusively for the members of the Rayaduvamsam family. It is not a public service and is not intended for use by the general public.

2. Data Collection & Messaging

We collect and store the following information to facilitate the chat service:

  • Display Name: The name you choose to identify yourself in the chat.
  • Messages: The text, images, and videos you send.
  • Timestamps: When messages were sent.

All data is stored in our private database (Supabase). While we take measures to secure the database, please be aware that messages are stored in plain text or standard database formats and are not end-to-end encrypted.

3. Usage Guidelines

By using this service, you agree to:

  • Only share content that is appropriate for a family setting.
  • Not share the "Family Password" with anyone outside the family.
  • Understand that the administrator (creator) has access to the database for maintenance purposes.

4. Media & File Uploads

Images and videos shared on the platform are uploaded to a third-party storage service. Do not upload sensitive personal documents (like financial records, government IDs, etc.).

5. Contact

For any questions or to request data deletion, please contact the family administrator directly.
